# BK: Breaking Barriers: My Battle with Depression and Self-Discovery

#### Overview

Navigating teenage depression was the most challenging period of my life. This story delves into the triggers and manifestations of my depression, coping mechanisms, critical moments of self-discovery, and the ongoing mental health journey.

#### The Triggers and Manifestations of Depression

My depression stems from a childhood dominated by fear of my father's strict and violent discipline. An accident that left me handicapped at ten added more trauma. The physical pain was immense, but the emotional toll was greater. As a teenager, these experiences became overwhelming, leading to hopelessness and despair.

My academic and extracurricular responsibilities were heavy and self-imposed. I had to maintain straight A's, work long hours on the farm and in my father's oil and gas business, and navigate adolescence. Imagine never receiving a compliment until your 20’s? The pressure to excel and the lack of a supportive outlet pushed me into a dark place. Cutting became a coping mechanism—a way to exert control over my pain and release tension.

#### Coping Mechanisms and Key Moments of Self-Discovery

Initially, my coping mechanisms were destructive. Cutting provided temporary relief, but it was a dangerous way to manage my emotions. The turning point came in my senior year when I began to question my goal of becoming a plastic surgeon—a goal I had clung to since the accident.

My trusted surgeon, Dr. Wagner, shattered my aspirations by advising against a medical career due to the industry's changes. This forced me to reevaluate my future but opened the door to self-discovery and exploration as I started my journey.

I immersed myself in philosophy, seeking answers to existential questions. The study provided a new perspective on life, helping me understand my experiences and emotions. It was a slow and painful process, but I gradually learned healthier ways to cope with my depression.

#### The Ongoing Journey of Mental Health

My mental health is ongoing. The battles with depression persist, but I've developed tools and strategies to manage them. Meditation, mindfulness, and personal growth are integral to my life. I self-medicated in college, but with friends' help, I found the good in the bad.

Building Fielding Systems and later ventures like agMD and EnergiAcres provided a sense of purpose and fulfillment that was previously missing. These projects allowed me to channel my energy and creativity into something positive, turning my past struggles into strength and resilience.

#### Lessons Learned

My battle with depression taught me the importance of self-awareness, seeking help, and resilience. It reinforced the belief in finding a path forward, even in the darkest times. By embracing my experiences and using them as a foundation for growth, I overcame my barriers and created a meaningful life.
