# BK: How to Turn 40 Surgeries into a Factory for Grit

#### Overview

Enduring 40 surgeries throughout my life, starting with a traumatic accident at age ten, has built unparalleled resilience and grit. This narrative explores how these experiences have shaped my character, instilled a relentless drive, and turned pain into a powerful catalyst for growth.

#### The Initial Trauma and Its Aftermath

On an autumn day in 1990, my life took a drastic turn when an ATV accident left me with severe injuries, leading to grueling hospital stays, surgeries, and a battle to regain mobility. The first surgery was just the start of a long, arduous journey testing my physical and mental limits.

#### Facing Each Surgery: A Test of Resilience

Each of the 40 surgeries presented unique challenges, requiring immense strength and determination to recover from skin grafts, muscle flaps, and reconstructive procedures. The physical pain was excruciating, but the emotional and psychological toll was equally demanding.

I vividly remember learning to walk again after a major surgery. My brain struggled to accept my damaged foot. I fell over the first step. This process was repeated numerous times, demanding grit and perseverance. The moment I saw blood soaking through my cast after stepping on my injured foot during a game of keep-away, I knew I had set myself back months. This cycle of progress and setbacks became a relentless teacher.

#### Turning Pain into Purpose

The constant surgeries and recoveries forced me to develop coping mechanisms and mental fortitude. I realized wallowing in self-pity would hinder my progress, so I channeled my pain into a relentless drive for improvement.

My passion for technology emerged as a beacon during dark times. The family computer became my escape, a tool for rebuilding my identity. I dove into learning and mastering new skills, turning pain into a catalyst for growth. This passion for technology became the foundation for my entrepreneurial ventures.

#### Building a Factory for Grit

Enduring 40 surgeries taught me valuable lessons about resilience, perseverance, and the power of a determined mindset. These experiences turned me into a grit factory, constantly refining my ability to face challenges and emerge stronger.

1. **Embracing the Process** : Each surgery reminded me that progress comes in small, painful steps. Focusing on incremental improvements helped me stay motivated.

2. **Mental Toughness** : The pain and setbacks taught me to disassociate from immediate discomfort and focus on the long-term goal. This mental toughness became a cornerstone of my character.

3. **Adaptability** : Every setback required me to adapt and find new ways to overcome obstacles. This has been crucial in my professional life, allowing me to navigate the ever-changing tech industry.

4. **Relentless Drive** : The surgeries instilled a relentless drive to succeed. I learned that giving up was never an option, and this determination fueled my entrepreneurial ventures.

#### Applying Grit in Entrepreneurship

The lessons from my surgeries have been crucial in building and scaling my companies. The resilience and perseverance I developed during recovery translated into my work ethic and approach to business challenges.

Building Fielding Systems from the ground up required the same grit and determination as recovering from surgery. The ability to push through long hours, stay focused on the end goal, and adapt to changing circumstances were skills honed during my years of medical battles.

#### Conclusion

Turning 40 surgeries into a factory for grit has defined my life. The pain and challenges shaped me into a resilient and determined individual, capable of overcoming any obstacle. These experiences drive my entrepreneurial success and guide me in all aspects of life.
