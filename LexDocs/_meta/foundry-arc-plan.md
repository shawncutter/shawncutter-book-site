## THE DIAGNOSIS

### What You Have

The Foundry arc contains 17 drafts that split cleanly into **two parallel tracks** that currently sit side-by-side without connective tissue:

| Track | Stories | Words | Feel |
|-------|---------|-------|------|
| **Personal / Inner Game** | 8 stories | ~8,600 | Raw memoir — family, therapy, identity, depression |
| **Technical / Outer Game** | 7 stories | ~4,900 | EnergiAcres vision docs — pads, gas, compute, digital twins |
| **Bridge / Both** | 2 stories | ~700 | Profile + future list (reference material, not narrative) |

**The problem:** The technical pieces read like white papers, not memoir. The personal pieces are outstanding but float without a timeline anchor. None of the 17 stories have a `date_event` set. The arc has no chronological spine.

### What's Missing

1. **The Origin Scene** — How did EnergiAcres actually start? There's no "founding moment" story. We know Cedar Valley Farms, but when did the energy campus idea crystallize? What was the napkin moment?

2. **The Mansfield Story** — Referenced in multiple pieces but never told. Why Mansfield? What happened when you first walked the site?

3. **The Cedar Valley Farms Reckoning** — "The Day I Stopped Recruiting My Family" and "The Quiet Quit of Legacy" are powerful, but there's no setup piece that shows CVF in its *good* phase before it fractured. We need the "before" to feel the "after."

4. **The Bridge from Forging → Foundry** — Fielding Systems exit, Dad's death aftermath, the space between corporate success and "now what?" This is the connective tissue between Arc 3 and Arc 4.

5. **Gia's Role** — Your daughter is listed as a key person in the CLAUDE.md but barely appears in Foundry. The "legacy" arc should include what you're building *for* her.

6. **The Current Moment** — Where are you right now? March 2026. What does the day look like? What's unresolved? The arc needs a "present tense" anchor.
