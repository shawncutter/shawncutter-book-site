# Family Recording Questions — P2L Book Project
*Multiple viewpoints on the same life. Each person gets their own set of questions tailored to what they witnessed, what they carry, and what they'd say if someone finally asked.*

---

## 📋 HOW THIS WORKS

1. **Give each person their question set** (printed or texted)
2. **They record voice memos** on their phone — no prep, no script, just talk
3. **They send them back** (text, email, AirDrop, whatever)
4. **We process them** the same way we process Shawn's — extract facts, emotional detail, corrections, and new material
5. **Integrate into the book** — not replacing Shawn's voice, but adding dimension. Confirmation, contradiction, and the details only someone else would remember.

**Ground rules to share with them:**
- There are no wrong answers
- If you don't remember, say "I don't remember" — that's useful too
- If it gets emotional, keep going or stop and come back — either is fine
- Say what YOU remember, not what you think happened
- 2-5 minutes per question is plenty

## 👩 BETH (Mom) — Elizabeth "Beth" Cutter

*Beth is 67. She's the quiet center of this entire story. She saw everything — Chuck's intensity, the accident, the surgeries, the boys fighting, the farm holding together and falling apart. She's the one who kept the room standing.*

### The Accident & Hospital
1. **October 29, 1990.** Where were you when you found out? Who told you? What do you remember about that first hour?
2. **The hospital.** How long were you there? What did the doctors tell you? What did you NOT tell Shawn?
3. **The surgeries.** 40 surgeries over years. What was that like as a mother — driving your kid to be cut open again? Did it ever become routine, or was it always terrible?
4. **Did Shawn change?** Before the accident vs. after. What did you see shift? Was it gradual or overnight?

### Chuck
5. **How did you meet Chuck?** What was he like when you were young?
6. **Chuck as a father.** What was he good at? What was he bad at? What do you wish he'd done differently?
7. **The hard parts.** You don't have to go deep, but — what was the hardest thing about being married to Chuck Cutter?
8. **Shawna.** What was that like for you? How did you find out?
9. **Chuck's death.** November 12, 2010. Where were you? What happened? What do you remember about that day and the days after?

### The Boys
10. **CJ and Shawn together.** What do you remember about them as kids? The fighting, but also the good. The forts, the animals, the fair.
11. **CJ and Shawn apart.** They don't talk now. What do you think happened? What do you wish for them?
12. **Who was Shawn as a kid?** Before the accident. What was he like? What did he care about? What made him laugh?

### The Farm
13. **The Bechtel farm.** What does this land mean to you? How far back does it go? Why won't you sell it?
14. **Cedar Valley.** What was your experience of Cedar Valley Farms? What worked? What didn't? How did it feel when it fell apart?
15. **The yard.** Shawn says you're still out there at 67, blowing off the yard, tending the garden, working the acreage. Why? What keeps you out there?

### For the Book
16. **If you could say one thing to Shawn** that you've never said — or that you've said but he didn't hear — what would it be?
17. **What are you proud of?** About him, about yourself, about this family.

## 👨 CJ (Older Brother)

*CJ is the other half of "the Cutters outside." The shared bedroom, the forts, the fighting, the farm work. He's the one who called at 3:30 AM when Chuck died. The relationship is currently blocked — these questions assume willingness to participate, which may not exist.*

### Growing Up
1. **The bedroom.** You shared a room with Shawn for years. What do you remember about it? The water beds, the TV, the fights?
2. **The forts.** You built them together. What do you remember about building them? Whose idea was it? What was the best one?
3. **The fighting.** You were bigger. He was smaller. What do you remember? Did it feel fair? Did it ever go too far?
4. **Working together.** The farm, the animals, the fair. What was your experience of the work? Did you resent it or did you like it?

### The Accident
5. **October 29, 1990.** Where were you? How did you find out? What do you remember about that day?
6. **After.** How did Shawn's accident change YOUR life? What shifted for you?
7. **The wrestling tournament.** 3-4 months after the accident, Shawn came back. You got 2nd place that day. What do you remember?

### Chuck
8. **Your relationship with Dad.** What was it like? Different from Shawn's?
9. **November 12, 2010.** You made the call at 3:30 AM. What happened that night? What do you remember?
10. **The funeral.** You spoke. What did you say? What do you remember about that day?

### Now
11. **What happened between you and Shawn?** From your perspective. What broke?
12. **If you could say one thing to him** — recorded, not face to face — what would it be?

## 👩 CARINA (Wife) — Carina Cutter (b. 7/8/1981)

*Carina has been with Shawn since Miami of Ohio. She's seen the moped, the elopement, the move to Florida, Fielding, Quorum, the kids, and the therapy. She knows the version of Shawn that exists at home — which is different from the version the world sees.*

### The Beginning
1. **How did you meet Shawn?** The real version. The handicap placard, the moped, the townies. What did you think of him?
2. **The elopement.** Whose idea was it? What do you remember about that day? What did your parents say?
3. **Moving to Florida.** 2005. What was that like? What did you leave behind?

### The Build
4. **Fielding Systems.** What was it like being married to someone building a company from nothing? What was the hardest year? What was the best?
5. **Quorum.** March 2015 — the Aria, the announcement. What do you remember? What changed after that?
6. **The exit.** December 2018. What was Shawn like after? Better, worse, different?

### The Kids
7. **Gia's birth.** 32 hours of labor. What do you remember? What was Shawn like in the room?
8. **Tessa's birth.** Different from Gia? How?
9. **Shawn as a father.** What's he good at? What does he struggle with? What do the girls see?

### The Hard Parts
10. **The emotional wall.** Shawn talks about not being able to feel things fully. When did you first notice it? What does it look like from your side?
11. **Cedar Valley / GreenScreens / Feb 2022.** What was that period like for your family? What was Shawn like during the "hardest and most painful time"?
12. **Therapy.** You started couples therapy in 2023. What made you both say "we need help"? What's changed since?

### For the Book
13. **What doesn't the world know about Shawn** that you think matters?
14. **"Incrementally better."** Shawn says you're incrementally better week by week. Do you agree? What does that look like from your side?
15. **If you could say one thing** for the book — something you want recorded — what would it be?

## 👧 GIA (Daughter, b. 2010) — IF/WHEN APPROPRIATE

*Gia is 15-16. She doesn't know about the book. These questions are for LATER — when/if Shawn decides to bring her in. They're gentle, open-ended, and don't require her to know the full story.*

### About Dad
1. **Describe your dad in three words.**
2. **What's your favorite thing to do with him?**
3. **Does your dad ever talk about growing up on a farm?** What has he told you?
4. **What do you know about your dad's accident?** Just what you know — no right answer.
5. **What do you think your dad is building with EnergiAcres?** In your own words.

### About You
6. **What matters to you right now?** School, friends, hobbies, whatever.
7. **Is there anything you wish your dad understood better about you?**

### For the Book (if she knows)
8. **If your dad was writing a book about his life, what do you think he should include?**
9. **What would you want to say in it?**
