## 🎭 WHO SHE IS

### The Quiet Center
- **"She's amazing. What she had to endure... eternal love in her heart."**
- The protector. Shielded the boys from Chuck's intensity at times.
- Unconditional love. Puts others first to her own detriment.
- "She's done enough — it's time for the kids to step up."
- At 67, still out there working the acreage because the plan to retire her (Cedar Valley) failed.

### What She Endured
- Chuck's intensity and dominance
- Chuck's infidelity (Shawna)
- Shawn's accident (October 29, 1990) — 40 surgeries over years
- CJ and Shawn fighting constantly
- Chuck's death (November 12, 2010)
- Cedar Valley failure — the plan to retire her didn't work
- She's STILL working. At 67.

### The Bechtel Heritage
- **CRITICAL:** The farm is Beth's family, not Chuck's. Bechtel family heritage. Same farm since Ohio was a state.
- This means the farm-to-tech-to-farm arc goes through MOM'S lineage, not Dad's.
- Shawn is sitting at this very farm in March 2026, building EnergiAcres.
- Beth won't sell the main Bechtel farm. Would part with the other farms.
