## 🎭 WHO HE WAS

### The Public Chuck
- **Dominant presence.** The loudest person in any room. The one who got the stories told about him.
- **Oilfield operator.** Ran Cutter Oil — trucks, rigs, field work. Shawn grew up riding in his truck, absorbing the language of day rates, call-outs, standby pay, AFEs.
- **Hard worker.** Expected the same from his boys. Farm chores, animal responsibilities, early mornings.
- **Intense.** Not gentle. Not soft. But effective.

### The Private Chuck
- **Motorcycle accident** left him with knee injuries that later contributed to his death (exacerbated blockage → stents → blood thinners).
- **Infidelity** — Shawna. Beth found out. This is one of the hardest threads in the Forged arc.
- **Getting softer as he got older.** Session 5 confirmed: "Chuck was getting softer as he was getting older. I was really looking forward to him being a grandpa."
- **Met Gia in May 2010** — had about 6 months with her before dying Nov 12.
- **Would have been a great grandpa** — a version of Chuck the family never got to fully see.

### Chuck as a Father
- **Took Shawn everywhere.** Rides in the truck, oilfield visits, wing spots (BW3, sports bars on I-71 in Cleveland).
- **Zero-G flight with Shawn** — went up in the Cessna, Shawn did full pre-flight, Chuck was "super quiet." Did zero-G's, spooked him. Went for wings after at a BBQ place (name unknown).
- **Expected toughness.** Farm work, independence, responsibility. Tractor pulls were "dad's thing." Boys had to sit through them.
- **Physical discipline.** Beth sometimes had to protect the boys from Chuck's intensity.
- **The wrestling tournament** — one of the few overtly proud moments (Shawn returning to wrestling ~3-4 months after the accident).

## 🔗 KEY RELATIONSHIPS

### Chuck ↔ Shawn
- Core relationship of the entire book. Every arc references Chuck.
- Shawn absorbed Chuck's operating system — the work ethic, the intensity, the oilfield knowledge that became Fielding's secret weapon.
- Chuck took Shawn up in a plane. The flight with Chuck is one of the emotional peaks of Forging.
- "I didn't start sounding like Chuck until the kids were maybe 4 or 5."
- Shawn was looking forward to Chuck being a grandpa. Never got that.

### Chuck ↔ Beth
- Beth endured Chuck's intensity, his infidelity (Shawna), and his death.
- She was sometimes the protector — shielding the boys from Chuck's worst moments.
- "Eternal love in her heart" despite everything.

### Chuck ↔ CJ
- Different dynamic than with Shawn. (Details sparse — CJ recording questions may fill this in.)
- CJ made the 3:30 AM call when Chuck died.
- CJ spoke at the funeral.

### Chuck ↔ Gia
- Met Gia May 2010. Had ~6 months.
- The version of Chuck that was softening — the grandpa they barely got to know.
