# Physical Description Prompts — Voice Memo Set
*Photos show what people look like. Your voice shows how they MOVED, how they occupied space, how they smelled. Record these alongside dropping photos in.*

---

## CHUCK CUTTER (Dad)
1. **Close your eyes and see Chuck.** What did he look like? How tall was he? What was his build? What did his hands look like?
2. **How did Chuck move?** Fast, slow, deliberate? Did he take up space or did space make room for him?
3. **What did Chuck wear?** Work clothes, dress clothes, what was his default? Boots? Hat?
4. **Chuck's truck.** What did the inside smell like? What was on the dash? What was the seat like?
5. **Chuck's voice.** Was it deep, gravelly, sharp? Did he yell or did he just get quiet when he was pissed? What did his laugh sound like?

## BETH (Mom)
6. **What does Mom look like?** Now, at 67. But also — what did she look like when you were a kid? How has she changed?
7. **How does Beth carry herself?** When she's in the garden, when she's at a family event, when she's upset. What's her posture? Her energy?
8. **Her hands.** Garden hands? Work hands? What do they look like?
9. **What does the kitchen/house smell like when Mom's in it?**

## CJ (Brother)
10. **CJ as a kid vs. now.** What did he look like when you were fighting in the bedroom? 20-25 lbs bigger — what did that look like? And what does he look like now?
11. **How did CJ move?** Was he a lumberer, a runner, a pacer?

## CARINA (Wife)
12. **The first time you saw Carina.** What did she look like? What was she wearing? What hit you?
13. **Carina at the chapel.** The dress that broke. What did she look like in it? What did the chapel look like?
14. **Carina now.** What does she look like in the morning, in the car, at dinner?

## GIA & TESSA
15. **Describe your daughters.** Not just physical — how they move, what they sound like, their energy. Who do they look like? Who do they act like?

## THE ENGINEERS
16. **Sasha Nelson.** What did Sasha look like? At the desk, in a meeting, when he was locked into a problem?
17. **Jeff Taton.** What does Jeff look like? How does he carry himself? Tool concert Jeff vs. CTO Jeff?
