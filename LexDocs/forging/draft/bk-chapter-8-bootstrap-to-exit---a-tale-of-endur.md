## The Start of Fielding Systems

First, how I came up with the name. The ‘Fielding’ was supposed To be the name of my first son, but I caught so much crap from my dad and wife that I got to thinking that this should be my first child, my company. So that is what I did and I think it was fitting. Once I use the name for a company, I would not be able to use it again for a person and that seem to work out for everyone.

### Starting the company and the 60/40 split

It was not a question whether or not Fielding was being built for cutter oil, that was the genesis of the company. I would sit on the phone and listen to all of the requirements that CJ had and I knew that if I solve them, we would make more money and I would be able to start a company. I translated his requirements into software that was usable for him and others. I was rapidly approaching the point which I needed to commit to working on Fielding full-time. Formation was at the top of the list and when the time came to figure out ownership, my older brother stepped up and offered, a 60/40 split in which he would get 40% of the company. This created a big rift between him and I that I was unwilling to back down because I could see how much time he had to continue putting into cutter oil and knew that he would not be able to put that effort into Fielding. People are always willing to account for equity with a much different measuring stick than those that are actually putting in the work. CJ got really pissed off because he said that he was being generous. generous with my company? That's right it does not make sense.
