# **Neighbor Dividend: Power, Food, and Quiet Hours** _Design for the people who live next to you._

Rain on sheet metal. The greenhouse glows at dusk. Across the fence, a porch light hums from a small subpanel we call the Neighbor Circuit. Inside: fridge, two lights, a router, phone chargers. Five kilowatts, max. The storm pops a tree onto the main feeder. Our pad islands. The house next door barely notices.

Community isn’t a line item. It’s a load profile. If we’re going to build where it runs, the design has to carry people, not just loads. We wire a protected neighbor subpanel for a ring of homes, tie produce credits to the greenhouse, and publish quiet hours and haul routes. Complaints close with timestamps. No secret inbox. No PR fog.

We drill it quarterly. Islanding cutover under 100 ms. BESS holds 40–70% SOC overnight. Gas sips, not chugs. Greenhouse CO₂ at 900 ppm in winter, 800 ppm in shoulder season. Quiet hour noise at the property line below 55 dBA. [NEEDS CHECK] If we miss, we log it and fix the corner first.

The turn came after the first real storm. One neighbor called. “The hum changed.” He was right—a fan skid ran outside the quiet window. We tuned the schedule and brought a produce crate the next day with the receipt. The complaint count matters, but the closure time matters more.

Farmer training taught us to walk fence lines on Sundays. Oilfield taught us call trees and muster points. Startup taught us to ship the page that earns trust. EnergiAcres ties it together: a neighbor hotline answered by a human, door‑hangers with islanding instructions, a map of haul routes that actually matches tires on gravel.

**AI tie.** AI coaches dispatch: it predicts when to pre‑charge the BESS before storms, schedules loud equipment outside quiet hours, and runs a weekly “neighbor audit” from sensors—noise, light spill, particulate. Sensor democracy: no single sensor is king; quorum voting avoids false alarms. Local‑first. The island must behave when the cloud can’t.

**Rule: Carry people, not just loads.**
