# BK: From Farm to Startup: The Journey of Building My First Company

#### Overview

Building Fielding Systems from the ground up was a business venture and a manifestation of my commitment to resilience, innovation, and hard work. This narrative explores the origins, challenges, turning points, and eventual success of Fielding Systems, highlighting the key lessons.

#### The Initial Idea and Inspiration

The seeds of Fielding Systems started in Ohio, where my family's oil and gas company operated. I grew up in energy production and farm life, which gave me insight into both industries. After dealing with inefficiencies in field data capture and production management, I saw an opportunity to revolutionize the sector using technology.

Inspiration came from my dual background: the work ethic I learned on the farm and the technical skills I honed through tinkering with computers and technology. By merging these experiences, I realized I could create solutions for real-world problems in the oil and gas industry. I was solving a problem that was personal to me.

#### The Challenges of Bootstrapping

Bootstrapping Fielding Systems was a significant feat. Every decision had to be strategic and cost-effective without external funding. My tech background was a double-edged sword; it equipped me with the skills to develop the software and meant I had to wear multiple hats—from coding and product development to sales and customer support.

Balancing startup demands with personal life was a big challenge. The long hours and pressure to deliver a functional product to industry standards were intense. My resilience and grit from my farm upbringing kept me pushing forward, even when the odds seemed insurmountable.

#### Turning Points

A turning point came when Penn Virginia, a major oil and gas player, adopted our production management and field data capture solution. It was make-or-break for Fielding Systems, and the pressure was on to deliver a seamless product. #### The Eventual Success and Acquisition

Fielding Systems' success was noticed. Our innovative approach to solving industry problems caught Quorum Software's attention, leading to an eight-figure acquisition in 2015. This validated the hard work and dedication poured into the company.

The journey continued. Integrating into Quorum Software brought new challenges, from navigating corporate culture to managing technical debt. Despite these hurdles, the acquisition allowed us to scale our solutions and impact the industry more broadly.

#### Lessons Learned

Building Fielding Systems taught me resilience, innovation, and adaptability lessons. It reinforced the belief that true success comes from the relentless pursuit of excellence and embracing change, even when venturing into uncharted territory.
