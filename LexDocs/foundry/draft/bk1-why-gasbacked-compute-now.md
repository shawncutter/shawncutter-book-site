**AI Playbook — Gas→SMR Bridge Planner**  **Purpose:** Underwrite a **10‑year** path from gas‑backed compute to SMR‑augmented campuses.

**Prompt:**

1. Map **gas interconnects** (pressure, volumetrics, tap/lateral feasibility) and standard **50–100 MW** blocks for \<36‑month delivery.

2. Inventory **stranded energy** opportunities (flare/cut sites, curtailed renewables) to pre‑stage **BESS** and demand response.

3. Produce a **grid‑help plan** : feeders to reinforce, sectionalizers, SCADA upgrades, and microgrid islands that reduce SAIDI.

4. Draft a **capture & reuse** ladder: immediate heat/CO₂ reuse; medium‑term capture pilot; long‑term integration points for SMR thermal/electric tie‑in.

5. Define a **skills pipeline** : local trade partners, QA certifications, and component shops that can pivot to SMR modules.

**Constraints:**

- Quarterly **emissions ledger** —scope, intensity, reuse offsets—publicly posted.

- **No bespoke one‑offs** : standardized blocks to keep timelines honest.

- **Community dividend** baked in (heat, CO₂, GPU credits) with metered KPIs.
