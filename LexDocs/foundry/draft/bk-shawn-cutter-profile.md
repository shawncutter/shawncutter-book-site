## Personal Journey & Mindset

- **Resilience** : How did the experience of undergoing 40 surgeries shape your outlook on life and your approach to business? It's only in hindsight that I see that my accident was the most critical event in the positive in my life. I was forced to get back up continually. Surgery after surgery put me back on crutches or in a wheelchair, and this happened for seven years. That is the type of resiliency you need as an entrepreneur.

- **Early Hustles** : How did your early entrepreneurial activities, like selling pizza slices, influence your business acumen? I was actually a hustler long before I sold pizzas and then computer repair in college. Growing up, I mowed yards, baled hay, scraped driveways, and washed semi trucks.

- **Outsider Perspective** : How has not fitting into any particular crowd in college shaped your approach to building relationships in your professional life? I believe this has shaped me by forcing me to have meaningful relationships with a few people, but building robustness in those relationships.
