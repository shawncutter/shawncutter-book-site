# BK: The Power of Grit: How Farming Taught Me to Persevere

#### Overview

Growing up on a farm instilled perseverance and grit. This story highlights how the values and lessons from farm life have shaped my work ethic and approach to challenges.

#### Daily Farm Responsibilities

Life on the farm was demanding. Responsibility was the expectation with daily chores and responsibilities from a young age. Mornings started early, often before dawn, to feed the livestock and prepare for the day's work. Evenings were spent tending to the animals, ensuring they were well-fed and cared for.

Farm life taught me the importance of consistency and dedication. Every task had to be completed, regardless of the weather or personal circumstances. This routine instilled a strong work ethic and a sense of responsibility that has stayed with me.

#### The Work Ethic Instilled by My Father

My father, Charles Cutter, was a demanding and harsh taskmaster who valued hard work and ensured his children understood its importance. His expectations often led to strict discipline for failure.

My father's drive pushed me to develop resilience despite fear and intimidation. The lessons from the farm—about hard work, perseverance, and seeing a task through—became the foundation of my approach to career challenges.

#### Specific Challenging Experiences

Acquiring and rehabilitating my great-grandparents' farm was one of the most challenging experiences. The property was in disrepair, needing extensive cleaning, repairs, and upgrades. The amount of work was daunting, but it taught me perseverance.

Managing the farm's 200 head of livestock was a significant experience. The responsibility of ensuring their well-being was immense. Farm life took a physical and emotional toll, but it forged resilience and determination that has served me well in my entrepreneurial ventures.

#### Translating Farm Lessons to Professional Life

The grit and perseverance developed on the farm have been essential in my career. Building Fielding Systems from the ground was dedication and hard work that farm life demanded. The ability to push through challenges, work long hours, and stay focused on the end goal were all skills honed during my time on the farm.

The resilience learned from farm life has been a crucial asset in the tech industry, where innovation and rapid change are constants. Adapting, staying persistent, and tackling problems head-on have enabled me to navigate the complexities of building and growing a successful business.

#### Lessons Learned

The power of grit and perseverance is a lesson that has stayed with me throughout my life. The experiences on the farm taught me that hard work and dedication are the keys to overcoming challenges and achieving success. These values have been the driving force behind my entrepreneurial ventures and continue to guide me in all aspects of my life.
