Hugh Palmer was like a grandfather to the Cutter kids. Hugh Palmer can (owning) Palmer and Son Excavating was a local construction company. Hugh would often spend storming nights at our place. Our house was up on top of the hill, and Hugh’s house was at the bottom. Any time Hugh needed an extra hand and school were not in; he knew he would get a reliable and good laborer if we came up to the Cutters.

I was always very interested if it meant that a piece of equipment would need to be operated on a job for some time. My spidey sense was constantly on high alert when Hugh started talking about positions that may need an extra hand. When the new Honda Dealership was built at the north end of our town, they needed a new parking lot, and to put it in correctly, it needed to have a sheep’s foot packer. This special machine consits of a large round drum with spikey things.

A sheep’s foot packer is got its name because it is a machine that vibrates a large drum filled with water. The purpose of this machine is to smaller surface area in order to compact the ground. This way, there is no additional settling after the foundation. Hugh had to rent this piece of equipment to do the job. Renting a selection of specialty equipment means the profitability of the job depends on one’s ability to use the equipment as well as getting it returned soon, since a daily rate charges it.
