# Welcome to the Farm

This is the beginning of my story on the farm where I grew up. The wide open fields stretched out in every direction, and the horizon seemed to go on forever.

My father Chuck would wake up early each morning and start his day with coffee on the porch. He was a man of routine, always deliberate in his movements and thoughtful in his words.

The old red barn stood weathered but strong, holding decades of memories within its wooden frame. Every corner had a story, every piece of equipment a purpose.

I spent my childhood running through those fields, learning how to work, how to think, and how to be a person in this world. The lessons I learned there have stayed with me my entire life.

Those early years shaped who I am today—resilient, focused on systems, and always looking for the underlying patterns in everything.
